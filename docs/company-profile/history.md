## Overview

Forge Financial Group is a fictional financial institution created as the foundation for a realistic enterprise networking and automation environment. While the company itself is fictional, its history is inspired by the evolution of many employee credit unions that grew into regional financial institutions over the course of several decades.

---

# Our Story

## 1938 — Humble Beginnings

Forge Mutual Credit Union was founded in 1938 by a small group of employees at Iron Creek Steel Works in Iron Creek, Pennsylvania.

At the height of the Great Depression, local steelworkers recognized the need for a financial institution that existed to serve working families rather than shareholders. Members pooled their savings to provide affordable loans, encourage responsible saving, and support one another during difficult economic times.

The organization began with a single office located inside the administration building of Iron Creek Steel Works and served fewer than 300 members during its first year.

Its founding principles were simple:

- People before profits
- Financial responsibility
- Community investment
- Long-term relationships

These principles continue to guide the organization today.

---

## 1955 — Serving the Community

As Iron Creek continued to grow, so did the credit union.

Membership expanded beyond steelworkers to include their immediate families, local businesses, and eventually residents throughout the surrounding communities.

New financial products were introduced, including:

- Home mortgages
- Auto loans
- Savings certificates
- Small business loans

The organization became known simply as **Forge Mutual**, reflecting its broader commitment to the community while honoring its industrial roots.

---

## 1978 — Regional Expansion

Forge Mutual opened its first branch outside Iron Creek and began serving neighboring communities across western Pennsylvania.

Throughout the 1980s, additional branches were established as the institution earned a reputation for conservative lending, exceptional member service, and long-term financial stability.

---

## 1994 — Commercial Banking

Recognizing the needs of local businesses, Forge Mutual expanded into commercial banking.

Services now included:

- Commercial lending
- Treasury management
- Business checking
- Cash management
- Equipment financing

Many of these businesses were suppliers, manufacturers, and logistics companies that had long supported the steel industry throughout the region.

---

## 2008 — Becoming Forge Financial Group

Following several successful mergers with community financial institutions throughout Pennsylvania and Virginia, the organization restructured under a new holding company:

# Forge Financial Group

The new name reflected the organization's expanded mission while preserving the heritage established seventy years earlier.

The "Forge" name serves as a tribute to the skilled workers and communities that built the institution.

---

## 2016 — Digital Transformation

Forge Financial Group began modernizing its technology platforms to support continued growth.

Major initiatives included:

- Virtualized data centers
- Modern core networking
- Private cloud services
- Enhanced cybersecurity
- Enterprise automation

The company's technology strategy shifted toward infrastructure as code, automation, and standardized network design.

---

## 2022 — Cloud First Strategy

To improve resiliency and accelerate application delivery, Forge Financial Group adopted a hybrid cloud strategy.

Key investments included:

- AWS
- Microsoft Azure
- Software-defined networking
- Infrastructure automation
- Enterprise observability

Critical customer-facing services were redesigned for geographic redundancy between the company's primary and disaster recovery data centers.

---
